Tracks

In addition to the general track, this year we will hold tracks around various topics, which will be managed by the various Free and Open Source Software communities that are active in Israel. The track list is still open - if you are a member of a community or meetup group focused on a topic related to Free and Open Source Software, and want it to be represented in the conference, please mail august@hamakor.org.il.

Code of Conduct

All attendees, speakers, sponsors and volunteers at our conference are kindly asked to adopt our code of conduct, and are required to agree to the Anti-Harassment Policy. We are expecting cooperation from all participants to help ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for everybody.
