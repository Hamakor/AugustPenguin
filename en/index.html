Code of Conduct

All attendees, speakers, sponsors and volunteers at our conference are kindly asked to adopt our code of conduct, and are required to agree to the Anti-Harassment Policy. We are expecting cooperation from all participants to help ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for everybody.
